About My Blog

ePro-Aggregator is a web-app that collects data from the ePro completed PR Masterlists from field offices and then consolidate them to enable PALM managers to run aggregate reports on the consolidated data.

ePro-Aggregator does not require field teams to re-enter the data; instead, it plugs into their existing workflows to get the data automatically. ePro-Aggregator integrates with Google Drive and Dropbox so when field teams store their PR masterlists on these platforms, they make their data accessible to ePro-Aggregator via backend API calls. However, an initial authorization needs to be granted for this process to work.
